When you contact AppiCore Solutions, we may collect your name, email address, the service you are interested in and any details you choose to share about your business need, idea or enquiry.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to understand your enquiry, reply to your message, discuss possible services and provide information about AppiCore Solutions.

The contact form opens your own email app with the message prepared. You can review the message before choosing whether to send it.
